About Emirates Powerlink Electromechanical LLC

We bring the latest innovative approach to contracting services.

Emirates Powerlink Electromechanical LLC is an Abu Dhabi based MEP contracting company.

We are proud to have sustained strong and long-term relations with our customers over the years while also being engaged in numerous prominent projects for high-profile clienteles across Abu Dhabi. Our success rests on building trusted, long-term client relationships, and our focus remains on delivering first class project execution, cost control and effective risk management, building on our track record in core markets to win new business and strengthen our position. Our goal is to ensure a fast, accurate and well-timed delivery of our services through our commitment and devotion to our customers.

ENGINEERING FACILITIES

Our engineering facility is fully capable of doing the design and build projects. Subjected to the project demand dedicated team may be stationed on site under the direct control of Project Manager, by maintaining the continuous interaction with the department to make sure the adherence of processes and standards.
